About Us

WHO WE ARE?

"ASTRA Robotics" is the Robotics Chapter of RV College of Engineering, Bangalore. We are Young and aspiring team of engineering students who are passionate about building robots and encouraging students to pursue their interests in this field.

WHAT WE DO?

Actively participate in various Robotics events held annually across the globe. We spread the knowledge of Robotics by conducting workshops, seminars and talks.

OUR VISION

To create a platform to do Research & Innovation in the field of Robotics and automation,with a focus on Opportunistic Learning.

OUR MISSION

Identify students who are interested in the field of robotics and automation and motivate them. Train students on elementary circuitry and design principles.

UNIVERSITY ROVER CHALLENGE

The University Rover Challenge (URC) is the world's premier robotics competition for college students. This is a challenge to design and build the next generation of MarsÂ rovers that will one day work alongside human explorers in the field. It is held annually in the desert of southern Utah in the United States where the conditions are an approximation for the conditions of the red planet.

This year University Rover Challenge is our ultimate goal. We have the blue prints for our Rover laid out. We have committed ourselves to go out there in the international stage and prove what we are capable of!

Why Support Us?

Astra Robotics is a multidisciplinary student group in R.V. College of Engineering, Bengaluru, with a commitment of breaking boundaries of innovation in Robotics and current technology.

We are constantly working on projects which have enriched us with experience in design, management and leadership. We not only learn from what we do but also we also take the initiative to spread the knowledge of Robotics by organising seminars, talks and workshops.

We are looking forward for Companies and Individuals who can Sponsor us in our endeavour to push innovation in Robotics. Sponsorships can be in the form of cash, gift-in-kind, reduces rates on hardware or even in form of technical assistance.
